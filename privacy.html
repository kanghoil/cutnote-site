Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Overview

CutNote is a desktop application that processes video and audio files entirely on your local computer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Data Processing

All video and audio processing is performed locally on your PC. Your media files, transcriptions, and subtitle data never leave your device. CutNote does not upload, transmit, or store any of your content on external servers.

Information We Collect

CutNote collects minimal information necessary for license management:

  • Email address — Used for license activation and subscription management through our payment provider (LemonSqueezy).
  • License key — Used to verify your subscription status.

We do not collect:

  • Video or audio content
  • Transcription or subtitle data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Device identifiers or tracking data
  • Browsing history or cookies

Third-Party Services

CutNote uses LemonSqueezy as a payment and subscription management provider. When you purchase a subscription, your payment information is handled directly by LemonSqueezy. CutNote does not process, store, or have access to your payment details (credit card numbers, etc.).

Please refer to LemonSqueezy's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle your data.

Data Storage

All application data (settings, job history, subtitle files, autosave data) is stored locally on your computer in your user profile directory. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the application or manually removing the data folder.

Children's Privacy

CutNote is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of CutNote after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
